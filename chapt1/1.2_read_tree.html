TOF绝对刻度的方法¶

$T_{Off}$PMT

方法¶

  • $t_u=TOF+(L-x)/v_{sc}+T_{uoff}$
  • $t_d=TOF+(L+x)/v_{sc}+T_{doff}$
  • $TOF=(t_u+t_d)/2 -L/v_{sc}-(T_{uoff}+T_{doff})/2$
  • C$TOF=(t_u+t_d)/2 +C$ #### $TOF_0=d_0*c$, $d_0$(), C.
  • $d_0=502.5$cm, $TOF_0=16.748 ns$
  • $TOF(0)=42.942$ ns
  • $C=TOF_0-TOF(0)=-26.194$ ns
